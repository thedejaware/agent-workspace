## Competitive Intelligence Tracking

### Information Sources

- [ ] **Competitor websites:** Reviewed all pricing, features, positioning
- [ ] **Product trials:** Signed up and tested products (if available)
- [ ] **Customer reviews:** Read 10+ reviews per competitor on G2/Capterra
- [ ] **Crunchbase:** Checked funding, team size, investors
- [ ] **LinkedIn:** Analyzed team size, growth, job postings
- [ ] **Social media:** Followed for product updates and announcements
- [ ] **Blog/changelog:** Reviewed recent updates and roadmap hints
- [ ] **Job postings:** Analyzed what they're building (engineering roles)

### Ongoing Monitoring Plan

- **Weekly:** Check competitor changelogs, social media announcements
- **Monthly:** Review new competitor reviews, job postings
- **Quarterly:** Full competitive analysis refresh, reassess threat levels

### Key Signals to Watch

- [ ] Competitor raises funding round (indicates aggressive growth plans)
- [ ] Competitor launches feature matching your differentiator (weakens your position)
- [ ] Competitor pivots toward your niche (direct threat increase)
- [ ] Competitor acquires competitor (market consolidation)
- [ ] New competitor enters with significant funding (market getting crowded)
